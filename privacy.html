Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Blackridge Ventures LLC (“Blackridge Supplies,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) collects, uses, and handles information submitted through this website. We are located at 7901 4th St N STE 300, St. Petersburg, FL 33702. For questions, contact us at purchasing@blackridgesupplies.com.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a supplier inquiry through this website, we may collect the following information: your name, email address, company name, website, product category, fulfillment model, MAP policy status, and any additional notes or product line details you choose to provide. We do not collect payment information or sensitive personal data through this site.

2. How We Use Information

We use submitted information solely for business purposes related to evaluating and establishing supplier relationships. Specifically, we use your information to respond to supplier inquiries, evaluate partnership and category fit, coordinate onboarding documentation, and maintain business communications related to an active or prospective supplier relationship.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes. We may disclose information only as required by applicable law, legal process, or regulatory obligation, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Blackridge Ventures LLC or others.

4. Data Retention

Supplier inquiry data is retained for up to two (2) years from the date of submission for legitimate business purposes, including follow-up, documentation, and partnership evaluation. After this period, data may be deleted or anonymized unless an active business relationship requires continued retention.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact us at:
